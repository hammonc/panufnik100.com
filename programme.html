2.30pm, Hall 1
Dreamscape
Heather Shipp, Clare Hammond and members of the Brodsky Quartet

Songs: Mine Eye and Virtue by Roxanna, Dreamscape by Andrzej and Sweeter than Roses by Purcell. Works for piano: Modlitwa by Andrzej and Roxanna, 12 Miniature Studies, Nos. 7-12 by Andrzej, and an arrangement of three movements from Andrzej's Hommage à Chopin by Roxana. Piano Trio by Andrzej Panufnik.

4.30pm, Hall 2
My Father, the Iron Curtain and Me
A screening of the 2009 film introduced by Norman Lebrecht

The film charts the record producer and graphic artist Jem Panufnik’s attempts to discover more about his father’s life in Poland and dramatic escape to Britain in 1954. Following the screening, there will be an audience Q&A, hosted by Norman Lebrecht, and a discussion of Sir Andrzej Panufnik’s life and work with his widow, Lady Camilla Panufnik and their children, Roxanna and Jem.

6.30pm, Hall 1
O Vos Omnes
Brodsky Quartet

Purcell Fantasia, Andrzej Panufnik String Quartet No. 1, Gesualdo and Roxanna Panufnik String Quartet m. 1 O Tu Andrzej [LONDON PREMIERE], Andrzej Panufnik String Quartet No. 2, Mozart Adagio and Fugue K. 546, Roxanna Panufnik String Quartet m. 2 [LONDON PREMIERE], Andrzej Panufnik String Quartet No. 3

9pm, Hall 2
Cabaret at the Warsaw 'Sztula i moda' Café
Singer tbc, the Brodsky Quartet and Clare Hammond

Popular Polish songs from the 1930s by Andrzej Panufnik, Witold Lutoslawski (writing as Derwid), Roxanna Panufnik and Gershwin. Polish drinks and snacks to be served at the bar.

All day, Foyer
Photographic Exhibition
With pictures by Camilla Panufnik, FRPS

Throughout the day, an exhibition of photographs of Sir Andrzej Panufnik, both as composer and family man, by his professional photographer wife, Lady Camilla Jessel Panufnik FRPS will be on display in the Foyer.
