2.30pm, Hall 1
Love Song
Heather Shipp, Clare Hammond and members of the Brodsky Quartet

Songs: Mine Eye and Virtue by Roxanna, Love Song by Andrzej and Sweeter than Roses by Purcell. Works for piano: Selection from 12 Miniature Studies by Andrzej, and an arrangement of three movements from Andrzej's Hommage à Chopin by Roxanna. Piano Trio by Andrzej Panufnik.

4pm, Hall 1
My Father, the Iron Curtain and Me
A screening of the 2009 film hosted by Norman Lebrecht

This screening of the 2009 film My Father, The Iron Curtain and Me, directed by Krzysztof Rzaczynski will be introduced by Norman Lebrecht. The film charts the record producer and graphic artist Jem Panufnik’s attempts to discover more about his father’s life in Poland and dramatic escape to Britain in 1954. Following the screening, there will be an audience Q&A, hosted by Norman Lebrecht, and a discussion of Sir Andrzej Panufnik’s life and work with his widow, Lady Camilla Panufnik, their children, Roxanna and Jem, and the director of the film, Krzysztof Rzacynski.
